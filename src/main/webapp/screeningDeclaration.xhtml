I hereby authorize the SARS Vetting, Screening and Conflict of Interest Unit to forward any personal information, including my fingerprints, name and ID to verification information suppliers acting on behalf of SARS (including but not limited to background checks and screening companies, AFISwitch, the South African Police Services, the South African Criminal Record Centre, the Government of the RSA, and any relevant educational, training and credit organizations) for the purpose of verifying my personal credentials and records. I furthermore indemnify SARS and its verification information suppliers against any liability that may result from the bona fide furnishing and verification of my personal information for the sole purpose of verifying my personal credentials and records.

I furthermore authorize the SARS Vetting, Screening and COI Unit to obtain the relevant information where it concerns my tax status/affairs.

I understand that it is a condition of SARS’ verification information suppliers that this information is furnished by them solely for the purposes of my proposed / continuation of employment via the SARS Vetting, Screening and COI Unit.
